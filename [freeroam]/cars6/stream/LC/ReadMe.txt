Liberty City liveries for GTA V Vapid Taxi NYC.


INSTALLATION:
-------------
!! Don't forget to backup your files !!

1) Use OPENIV or SPARKIV to edit files

2) Open vehicles.img located in [your GTAIV folder]\pc\models\cdimages

3) Edit taxi.wtd

4) Replace corresponding vpint_sign_X.png textures (where X = 1 to 4)

5) Replace rom_stickers.png and taxi_sings_1.png

6) Save and rebuild

7) Enjoy...
